What all can you do in this ?

As an administrator:

  • Create an election.
  • Send the election to the voter's list!
  • Compute the tally.
  • Publish and release the results.

As a voter:

  • Vote in the elections assigned to you.
  • Verify that your votes has been cast correctly.
  • Can vote any no. of times (The only catch here is that Your Last Vote COUNTS!)
  • You will get all updates regarding the elections (assigned to you) in your E-mail ID.
